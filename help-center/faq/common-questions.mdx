## Overview

To add a new document in your documentation space:

1. Click the "New Document" button on your dashboard.
2. Enter a title and select a section.
3. Choose if the document should be public or private.
4. Click "Save" to create your document.

You can organize documents into sections from the sidebar. For further options, use the settings menu inside any document.
