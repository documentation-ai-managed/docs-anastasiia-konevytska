## Overview

Arrange your documents into sections for easy navigation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new section">
    ````text
    On the left sidebar, click "+ New Section".
    Enter a name (e.g., Project Plans, Meeting Notes).
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move documents into a section">
    ````text
    Drag and drop your documents into any section.
    Or, open a document and select "Move to section" from the menu.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder sections">
    ````text
    Click and drag sections up or down to change the order.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
