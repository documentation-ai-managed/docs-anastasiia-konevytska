## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An email address for account verification
- Stable internet connection

<Callout kind="info">

No credit card required for the free tier. Upgrade later for advanced features.

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`.

    Click the **Sign Up** button prominently displayed on the landing page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Fill in your email address, create a strong password, and provide your full name.

    Accept the terms of service and privacy policy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Anastasiia Konevytska.

    Click the verification link to activate your account. This takes under 1 minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://dashboard.example.com` and sign in with your credentials.

    You'll land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Project

Now set up a documentation space for your project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Project** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Basics" icon="settings">
    Enter a project name like `My First Docs`, select a visibility (public or private), and choose a brand color such as `#3B82F6`.

    Click **Create Project**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Project Ready" icon="check-circle">
    Your project dashboard appears. Note the project URL for sharing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate the Interface

The interface includes key sections for managing your docs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Dashboard" icon="home">
    Overview of all projects, recent activity, and quick actions like **New Doc**.

    Use the search bar to find existing content.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Projects" icon="folder">
    List view of your documentation spaces. Click any project to enter.

    Features bulk actions for archiving or exporting.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Documents" icon="file-text">
    Tree view of pages and folders within the active project.

    Drag-and-drop to reorganize structure.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
